## What I Built
- Interactive modules covering AI ethics, evaluation, and practical applications
- Community contribution system for users to submit resources
- Alignment with the CHOICE ACRL AI Literacy Framework
- Resources organized by academic discipline and use case

## Impact
Serving faculty, staff, and students across OU Norman campus, Health Sciences Center, and Tulsa campus with accessible AI education resources.

## Skills Used
LibGuides (Springshare), instructional design, information literacy, AI literacy education, resource curation


**[View the LibGuide →](https://guides.ou.edu/libai)**
